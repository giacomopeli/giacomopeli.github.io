Why I'm deploying a blog in 2022

My reasons and purposes of coding and starting a blog in English in 2022
3 January 2022 · 3 min · Giacomo Peli

Many times I have written pieces I have never published and many times I have taken photos I have never posted.

Writing#

At school teachers had always told me there was only a method to write. The introduction, where it is supposed to be the thesis, but you know, it’s chosen in advance by the documents the teacher gives you. So, you’re supporting a thesis that maybe you don’t agree with. The supporting paragraph, where you try to rewrite what you’ve just read in the documentation by making sure to change the words. And the conclusion, where you basically write once again what you’ve written in the introduction. Is the conclusion supposed to be as the introduction? No. And yes, you write about something you are not into. How many people continue to write after the school?

English#

We learn English for 5 years at primary school, 3 years at middle school and other 5 years at high school. There are a total of 13 years. At the end of this cycle you’re supposed to have high knowledge in English, but you can’t understand one hundred percent of a song or a film in English or, at most, you can, but with the help of subtitles and, moreover, in your own language. When you study a language for thirteen years, you are supposed to know it almost as your mother tongue, aren’t you? I mean, are you seeing how badly I wrote this post? I’ve passed four English certificates in my life, but, please, don’t tell anyone.

Coding#

In the first year of high school I learnt the basic knowledge of html, php, python and arduino. One of my classmates was a geek and he was able to code at the age 13 years. Having always been a computer science nerd I was fascinated by that. We became friends but, sadly, we went our separate ways very soon. For the period we were friends he taught me coding and we made a website from scratch. So, here I learnt the basics of html and php. Then the computer science teacher taught us python, I was only fourteen and a world opened up. For a few years due to a teenage crisis and stupid choices, I stopped learning coding. I studied chemistry and then management engineering. Now, I’m realising how coding skilss are important in our society. Recently, I’ve made a project for 3d printing written in python, but I want to post it as an online platform. That’s a small project, but I’m not yet able to do it. So I started this blog, more or less, coding and in the meantime I’m learning to do it. You can never can tell that this will be useful in my future projects. By the way, I’m trying to be noob as little as possibile: I’m not using wordpress.

What you’ll find here#

I have many ideas that are waiting to be written here. You can already find my Bookshelf in the menu. This is a section where I keep track of the books I read and that I would suggest to read. Probably I will search for a good method of rating.
